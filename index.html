About

After working as a bankruptcy paralegal for twelve years I decided maybe it was time to do something I might actually enjoy. It only took about an hour into my first day learning to code for me to realize I loved it, and I wish I had started twenty years ago. Better late than never though right?
After going into it thinking I'd really want to focus on back-end development, I've been surprised at how much I enjoy front-end development as well. Luckily that gives me the skill set, and drive, to be able to develop for the full stack as needed.
Now if I'm even luckier, and incredibly charming and talented, I'll get to keep doing this for a living. After all, if I'm not going to make websites with incredibly outdated Matrix header images, who will?
