About

After working as a bankruptcy paralegal for twelve years I decided maybe it was time to do something I might actually enjoy. It only took about an hour into my first day learning to code for me to realize I loved it, and I wish I had started twenty years ago. Better late than never though right?
After graduating from Eleven Fifty Academy in August of 2018 I started working at Preferred Population Health Management as a front-end developer.
I left there in March of 2019 and am now in independent web developer.
In my free time I have two daughters I'm raising to be giant nerds, and play far too many video games. I also love ancient egyptian things and would love to go see the pyramids someday. That's why that picture's up there at the top.
